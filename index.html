About us

At Sarfo Electricals, we are more than just an electrical store; we are your partners in powering your life. With a passion for innovation, quality, and customer satisfaction, we have been serving the Kumasi and beyond for 20 years. Our commitment to excellence has made us a trusted name in the electrical industry.

Our Story

Our journey over the year, Sole distribution and sales of KabelMetals, electrical fittings and lighting brands in the Ashanti Region . With a vision to provide top-quality electrical solutions to homes, businesses, and industries, Sarfo Electricals set out to create a store that would meet and exceed customer expectations. We have grown from a small shop to a thriving electrical emporium. Our dedication to staying at the forefront of technological advancements and industry trends has allowed us to expand our product range and services, all while maintaining our commitment to customer satisfaction.

Our Vision

Our vision is to continue being the go-to destination for all your electrical needs. We aim to empower our customers with the best electrical solutions, making their lives more efficient, sustainable, and enjoyable
